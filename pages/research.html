Explore the foundation of our website design. Here, we've listed all the sources and inspirations that guided the creation of our website.

Homepage

For the homepage, we took inspirations from epson and HP printers homepage. The sale and advertisement banners were inspired by those on the epson printers website. And the printers list component was inspired by the printers list componenent on HP printers homepage.

For the product details page, we took inspirations from xerox printers detail page. The layout and the theme of the page is similar to that of the xerox printers. About Us

When developing our website, we drew inspiration from Digital Marmalade and were particularly captivated by their flip-card function. We decided to create our own version, and also included a timeline to showcase our company's journey, much like Digital Marmalade's.

Digitalmarmalade our story page

About Our Company

In addition, we took inspiration from printer websites like Epson for our company page. We studied their design elements, layout, and overall aesthetic to gather ideas and create a professional-looking page. By taking reference from these websites, we were able to create a company page that is both visually appealing and informative for our visitors.

Epson's about our page

Ink Finder

During the development of our website, we were particularly impressed by Epson's ink finder function, which allows customers to easily find the right ink for their printer. We recognized the value of this unique concept and decided to incorporate it into our own website. To ensure accuracy and functionality, we invested time in gathering information about various printer series and their corresponding model numbers and ink type codes.
